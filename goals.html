Goals For The First Year Of Presidency

Anti-Discrimination Laws

It is appalling that there are still entire groups of people in the US who are being discriminated against. I will devote as much time and resources as necessary while in the Whitehouse to guarentee that nobody will ever again be mistreated just because they married a parrot!

Reform Segregated Schools

Many refuse to believe that there is still segregation in our public schools, but it is still a major issue that my family has personally had to deal with. My very own children were not accepted into Kindergarden solely because of their species! This is not what Rosa Parrots and Macaw Luther King fought for!

Bolster Our Agriculture

With the recent drouts and storms across the country, our farmers are sure to be suffering. If they are not assisted soon, we may soon need to increase imports on many of the important food products, like bird seed.

Improve Foreign Relations

The United States does not have as sturdy a relationship with certain foreign powers, and not allying ourselves closer with such nations could spell trouble for our nation, and the things we hold dear. That is why I must improve our relationship with arguably the most important foreign nation: New Zealand, home of the Kakapo parrots!
